Our Story

Hyderabad's
Stone Atelier

"Every stone carries the memory of centuries. We simply help it find its rightful place in your space."

ALV Stones was founded on the belief that premium stone is not merely a material — it is a statement of devotion, art, and intent. Our atelier in Hyderabad works with master craftsmen to translate raw stone into sacred and architectural works of art.

From intimate home temples with handcrafted Ganesha statues to grand commercial lobbies with monumental Buddha sculptures, our stone tells stories that endure through generations. Every piece is made-to-order with attention to the buyer's vision.
